What it does

Weekly Fetch pulls your favourite subreddits, Bluesky accounts, Tumblr blogs, Instagram profiles, and Mastodon accounts on a schedule you set, then presents the best posts as a distraction-free flashcard digest — one card at a time, no infinite scroll.

Notes you write per card are saved to a local SQLite database. Your data never leaves your machine.

l Next card
j Scroll down
