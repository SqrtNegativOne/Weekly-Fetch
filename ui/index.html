What it does

Weekly Fetch pulls your favourite subreddits, Bluesky accounts, Tumblr blogs, Instagram profiles, Mastodon accounts, and Twitter/X feeds on a schedule you set, then presents the best posts as a distraction-free flashcard digest — one card at a time, no infinite scroll.

Notes and todos you write per card are saved to a local SQLite database. Your data never leaves your machine.
